How it works

  1. Paste a video URL (YouTube, Vimeo, MP4, etc.).
  2. Our service extracts the audio and runs Whisper transcription.
  3. GPT‑4 creates a 2‑3 sentence summary.
  4. Copy the summary or share it instantly.
