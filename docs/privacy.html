Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how VideoSum AI (the "Service") collects, uses, and protects any information you provide when using the service.

Information Collected

  • Video URL submitted by the user (used only for audio extraction).
  • Transient audio files and transcription text (processed in memory and deleted immediately after summarisation).
  • IP address and request metadata for security and rate‑limiting purposes.

How We Use the Data

  • To download audio, generate a transcript, and produce a summary.
  • We do not store any video content, audio files, or generated summaries beyond the request lifecycle.
  • Aggregated usage statistics may be collected for improving the service.

Data Sharing & Disclosure

We never sell or share personal data with third parties, except when required by law.

Security

All processing occurs on secure servers with TLS encryption. Temporary files are deleted immediately after use.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the service constitutes acceptance of any changes.
